Inspiration

Every day, new headlines and events tear people and communities apart; socially through differing political views, and physically through an increasing frequency of natural disasters.

Freebie Magnet hopes to bring communities back together through the one thing no one can turn down: free stuff. By tracking events with free swag near you, Freebie Magnet hopes to "attract" communities back together through the power of freebies.

What's Next

Look forward to new features including event and host rating, tagging favorite hosts, and favorite tags. Also, keep an eye out for Freebie Magnet coming to your local Android or iOS device.
